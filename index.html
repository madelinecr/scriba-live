ScribaLive is a web application for college students to take, manage notes, and collaborate with peers in real time


Features

  • University aware, find your college easily in our database or add it yourself.
  • Enroll in your specific courses and take live notes with other students on your campus.
  • Use our real time editor to share and cross compare your notes with other students.

Technologies

  • Ember.js
  • Node.js
  • Express.js
  • Socket.io
